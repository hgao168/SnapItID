How it works

1. Pick country & doc

We fetch live rules from our Cloudflare Worker (size, background color, smiling, glasses).

2. Capture or upload

Use your phone or laptop camera, or upload an existing photo. Everything runs in your browser.

3. Background removal

An on-device AI segmentation model isolates your head and shoulders.

4. Auto crop & download

The image is recomposed onto the required background color and exported at 300 DPI ready for print.
